Selected Projects

Project 01

DUAL ARM System with FR-5 Robot Arms

Customer: KEPCO KDN

Developed an in-house dual-arm robotic platform with motor and linear actuators for precise arm positioning, along with safe electrical circuits for tele-operation power supply.

  • Analyzed existing dual-arm systems; selected mechanical and electrical components.
  • Designed sheet-metal parts: actuator cover, sensor kit enclosure, front housing.
  • Implemented step-servo motor control in Python with CW/CCW, speed, and E-Stop GUI.
  • Completed industrial relay-based electrical wiring and connections.
